Our Vision

Our vision is simple yet profound: to create an environment where every child can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. We envision a future where each child develops a lifelong love for learning, equipped with the skills and confidence to navigate the world with curiosity and compassion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a holistic educational experience rooted in the Montessori philosophy. We aim to cultivate independence, creativity, and a sense of responsibility in each child. Through hands-on learning, individualized instruction, and a supportive community, we empower our students to reach their full potential and become active contributors to society.

About us

Whether you opt for our Half-Day, Full-Day, or Extended Day Care Program, rest assured that your child will receive the highest standard of care and education at SIKSHANA. We believe in empowering young learners to reach their full potential, one enriching experience at a time.

Our approach

At SIKSHANA, we embrace the principles of Maria Montessori, pioneering educator and advocate for child-centered learning. Our classrooms are carefully prepared environments designed to stimulate exploration and discovery. We respect each child as a unique individual and tailor our curriculum to meet their interests, abilities, and developmental needs.

Our Community

We recognize that education is a partnership between school and home. We foster open communication and collaboration with parents and guardians, inviting them to be active participants in their child’s educational journey. Together, we form a supportive community where every voice is valued and every contribution is celebrated.

Choose what works best for your family

Whether you opt for our Half-Day, Full-Day, or Extended Day Care Program, rest assured that your child will receive the highest standard of care and education at SIKSHANA. We believe in empowering young learners to reach their full potential, one enriching experience at a time.

Half-Day Program

Our Half-Day Program provides a dynamic morning curriculum packed with engaging activities tailored to inspire young minds. From 9:00 am to 12:30 pm, your child will immerse themselves in hands-on learning experiences rooted in the Montessori philosophy, setting the stage for a lifetime of curiosity and exploration.

(9:00 am - 12:30 pm)

Full-Day Program

For families seeking a more comprehensive educational experience, our Full-Day Program extends learning opportunities until 4:00 pm. In addition to our enriching morning curriculum, your child will benefit from extended exploration, outdoor play, and specialized activities designed to foster creativity and social development.

(9:00 am - 4:00 pm)

Extended Day Care

We understand that balancing work and family commitments can be challenging, which is why we offer an Extended Day Care Program on an on-demand basis. With daily charges applicable from 4:00 pm to 6:00 pm, this program provides a safe and nurturing environment for your child to unwind, engage in supervised activities, and enjoy quality time with friends.

(4:00 pm - 6:00 pm)

To learn more about our flexible Montessori programs or to schedule a visit, please contact us today. We look forward to partnering with you on your child's educational journey.
